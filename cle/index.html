CLE Course Credit Information for CLEs in Minnesota

The Minnesota Board of Continuing Legal Education has an online reporting and sponsor integrated site (OASIS) where you can report your attendance at CLEs, even if you do not know the credit code! Please visit Minnesota Board of Continuing Legal Education. Follow the prompts to search the CLEs, and enter the information you have about the CLE. TIP: It helps to use the sponsor field and date, instead of the title field, because the search will not work unless your search includes exactly the same title information as entered by the course sponsor.

Lunch

Lunch is available at midday CLEs only. Choices are listed on CLE details page. Usually the lunch choices are: turkey sandwich, ham and cheese sandwich, roast beef sandwich, tuna salad sandwich, veggie sandwich, Caesar salad, or mixed greens mandarin salad.

Lunch orders made less than 24 hours in advance cannot be guaranteed.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations are accepted up to 24 hours in advance. If you do not cancel, you will be invoiced.
