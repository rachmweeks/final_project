ANNUAL EVENTS

Throughout the year, RCBA sponsors numerous social, fundraiser, and community outreach events. These include the annual Judges’ Dinner, Memorials, Annual Meeting, Golf Tournament, Families First Picnic, Bench & Bar Benefit, and Holiday Party. Other events include monthly New Lawyers Happy Hours, Sidebar: Meet the New Judges Happy Hours, and Off the Record Dinners with judges from Ramsey County.

Judges’ Dinner

The Judges of Ramsey County showcase their talents in a variety show and dinner held each March. The 2015 Judges' Dinner will be held on Thursday, March 26.

Memorials

The RCBA Memorials are held each spring to honor local attorneys and judges that have passed in the previous year. The 2015 Memorials will be held on Friday, April 17 at Hamline University’s Sundin Music Hall.

Annual Meeting

The RCBA Annual Meeting is held annually in May to recap the year and to honor those making a difference in Ramsey County.

Golf Tournament

Held in June, the annual Ramsey County Bar Foundation Golf Tournament is a fundraiser for the Foundation. The 2014 tournament raised nearly $15,000. The next tournament will be held on June 15, 2015 at Keller Golf Course in Maplewood.

Families First Picnic

Every summer, RCBA volunteer attorneys organize the Families First Picnic for more than 200 children and their family members from economically disadvantaged St. Paul neighborhoods. The next picnic will be held in July 2015.

Bench & Bar Benefit

The Bench & Bar Benefit, held in the fall of each year, is the major fundraiser for the Ramsey County Bar Foundation. Half of the money raised at the event is given directly to Southern Minnesota Regional Legal Services. The other half is awarded in grants to other law-related organizations. In 2013, the benefit raised nearly $60,000. The 2014 Benefit will be held on Saturday, November 1 at Crowne Plaza Hotel in St. Paul. Register today on the RCBA homepage!

Holiday Party

The RCBA Holiday Party is a social gathering held each December at a local St. Paul restaurant. The 2014 Holiday Party will be held on December 11 at Pool & Yacht Club.

New Lawyers Happy Hours

Every month, the New Lawyers get together at various local bars. These events are a great way to meet colleagues and also to network with seasoned legal professionals.

Sidebar: Meet the New Judges Happy Hours

This semi-annual happy hour is held to welcome the new judges of the Ramsey County District Court.

Off the Record Dinners

Enjoy a meal and informal conversation with a Ramsey County District Court or Appellate Court Judge at a local restaurant.
